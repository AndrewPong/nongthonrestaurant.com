Another Side Of You

"Students, may I have your attention," Mr. Lee beckoned as he stood tall with his balding head. Beside him, there was a girl with a bright smile standing. Ariel could not believe her eyes. "Today, we have a new student joining our class. She just transferred from Xing De. New student, would you like to introduce yourself."

The whole class looked at the new student with impressed faces. For sure they knew Xing De was a prominent elite school that only the rich could afford attending. It was more than fascinating to have a transferred student from there attending such a regular school in the district.

The girl beamed enthusiastically with strong sagacity of style. "Hi classmates! My name is Selina Ren. I know it will be a great year for us!"

Lots of boys whistled while the girls glared with envy. The girl was beautiful like a goddess and sweet like an angel. Her friendly tone had already won her numerous admirers. She was probably going to be a Hebe #2. The only difference was that she appeared a lot smarter. Ariel could only stare at her

Selina waved to her classmates as she stalked down the aisle for an empty seat. Out of all the seats she could have chosen, she opted for the one closest to Joe.

Ariel watched them; Joe's eyes gleamed with brightness as he welcomed her in his subtle ways. Apparently, he seemed as though he had already known it. Ariel felt disgusted at herself when she felt envy nibbling at her. For the past week, she hadn't seen Joe much and she didn't bother to look for him either.

Rainie poked Ariel in the side. "Wei, who is she?" The girl glanced at the duo in the back with tight observation, popping potato chips in her mouth. "I don't like her already."

"Rainie, you barely know her," Gillian pointed out.

"So what? She should know Joe is already taken. What is wrong with her? Ariel, you better do something before she snatches your boyfriend away," Rainie said warily.

Ariel sighed, grabbing some chips from Rainie's bag of chips. "They know each other."

"WHAT?!?!" Both Gillian and Rainie blared like horns, eyes completely widened.

Mr. Lee turned his head. "Is there something the matter?"

"No, Mr. Lee. You must have heard something from your head," Rainie said.

"Is that so?" Mr. Lee's eyebrows perked up in skepticism.

"Yes. Isn't that right, classmates?" Rainie glanced around, threatening all the students with her fiery eyes. They all responded yes to the teacher. Rainie was known as the alligator of the school, ready to bite your heads off if you don't waver to her commands. "See? Mr. Lee, maybe it's time for you to see a doctor."

"Ms. Yang, are you eating in my class?"

Rainie quickly hid her chips in her desk. "Ah, it must be your hallucination this time."

Ariel was trying her hardest to stifle her laughter from aside. Trust her friend to know how to make her laugh. Ariel felt so glad to have Rainie as a friend.

Mr. Lee turned his head around and Rainie instantly pulled her bag of chips out again, throwing several pieces in her mouth. "You're not serious, are you? How could the cold prince KNOW a girl? He usually pays them no attention!"

Ariel scribbled in her notebook. "How am I supposed to know?"

"Something is fishy," Rainie scrutinized. "Look at them talking. How could the Joe who is always so quiet talk in class?"

"I'd probably say she must be someone he likes from before," Gillian deduced.

Ariel's eyes perked as she shot her friend an amazed gaze. "H-how would you know?"

"Obviously, look at them. They're chatting as though they had known each other forever. Would you chat with a stranger like that? When the cold prince talks to a girl like that, there is obviously something between them. I don't think he even uses that kind of face when he talks to you," Gillian said.

Ariel was quaked in unease. Gillian was right. Joe had never acted in such a demeanor when he was with her.

"How did you know they knew each other, Ariel?" Rainie suddenly asked.

"I've seen them together before..." Ariel murmured.

"WHAT?! WHEN!?"

Mr. Lee turned back around once again. "Ms. Yang, Ms. Chung. I'm sure this is probably in my head again?" He asked, sarcastic.

"Stupid Mr. Lee. Two hours of detention? He must be joking me," Rainie cursed madly when the three were in the detention room later that day

"I have never gotten detention before! This is surely going to leave a stain on my perfect record!" Gillian exclaimed with horror.

"You guys shouldn't have opened your big mouths," Ariel said. "Even I got dragged down with you two. Mr. Lee isn't dumb as he appears to be."

"That old man. He's an imp."

"With a bald head," added Ariel.

"And stubby arms and legs," said Gillian.

"He probably eats children for breakfast."

The three girls looked at each other and giggled.

"Okay, I think we should devise up a plan," Rainie suggested, making the other two look at her with strange eyes. "I mean, about Ariel and Joe. Seriously, Ariel, you're not planning to sit back and let your boyfriend does whatever he wants, are you?"

"What is your plan?" Ariel asked, interested. Although knowing that it was wrong, she couldn't help but let curiosity devour her rationality. She, too, wanted to know what it was all about.

Rainie smirked smugly.

"Are you sure this is going to be fine?" Ariel asked a bit skeptically. "Do we have to wear these masks?"

"Of course we do. You want her to know who we are?" Rainie hissed as she fixed her hair. Clad in exaggerated janitors' outfits, the three looked outrageously into their roles. "No one will recognize us for sure."

"Really? Isn't it a bit too extreme? It might catch unnecessary attention," Ariel said, referring to their heavy aprons, head scarves, rubber boots and gloves, and three sets of mops and other things. They looked like spokespersons for cleaning supplies in a super market or advertisement.

"I'm more afraid of Mr. Lee catching us here," Gillian commented. "The detention monitor would surely find it odd we've been to the bathroom for so long."

"Hush! Here she comes," Rainie hushed. "Remember, once she turns around this corner, we snatch her and threaten her to spill everything!"

Indeed, there was Selina Ren, coming out of the school's auditorium with Joe by her side. The sight of them with full chemistry and smiles made Ariel's skin crawl with goose bumps and discomfort. For sure she didn't like the scene she was seeing.

"Damn it, why is that Joe with her!?" Rainie hissed.

Ariel could no longer fix her eyes on the duo. The sight had gotten her stomach churning to a point she could no longer stand it. "Forget it, let's just go."

"What?! No! I used up all of my saliva to sneak us out here, no way--"

"Well, well, well, look what we got here." Suddenly, a familiar voice snapped the girls' attentions. The voice was so damn recognizable that Ariel didn't need a second to pinpoint who its owner was.

"Mike He!" Rainie exclaimed with utter disgust, forgetting that she was in disguise. "How dare you show up! Didn't Ariel make it clear to you?"

Mike's smirk was the epitome of all arrogance. "As I expected. Of course, in this school, who else but our three musketeers would do such a stupid thing like disguising as some janitors?"

"Why you!" Rainie hissed, jerking up her mop and ready to whack him with it but Mike stepped back in awareness.

"Whoa, don't do anything rash and life threatening here. I have witnesses." With a snap of his fingers, a group of wild-looking rebels appeared from the corner. Clad in leather and heavy shoes, they resembled a bunch of thugs belonged in gangs.

"What do you want?" Ariel finally found her voice to bawl. She stepped in front of Rainie and glared up at Mike with intense resentment. Nothing in the world could describe the revulsion she bore for this human being.

"Oh Ariel," Mike said in a sweet but mocking tone. "Have you forgotten what you did to me?"

"You're such an insignificant thing to me that I don't have a frigging idea of what I did to you." Ariel spat with obduracy.

There was an offended spark in his eyes but it disappeared the second it emerged. "You've gotten feistier, babe," he said, touching her mask. She stumbled backwards and slapped his hand away. "No wonder Joe didn’t really fall for you.”

“For your information, they are really going out!” Rainie snapped. “They’re in LOVE!”

“Didn’t you say it was fake--”

“Hahaha! I was never aware that Mike, the girl’s so-called heartthrob, was a turtle!” Rainie looked belittlingly at him.

Mike’s eyes flashed. “I don’t give a damn if they’re really going out or not.” He turned to Ariel. “Remember I said you’re going to regret what you did to me Ariel?” He smiled mischievously before snapping his fingers and summoning his men.

Rainie, Gillian, and Ariel stumbled backwards as the gang advanced on them.

"Wei! What do you think you're doing! This is school ground! You'll get caught for whatever you plan to do!" Rainie blared, nervousness eating her up. Those muscles on the guys looked even bigger than her head.

"Oh, you're right." Mike said, with a smirk.

Before they knew it, they were being bagged in sacks and carried elsewhere. The sun immediately blinded them when they were released from the suffocating sacks.

"What the hell do you want, spit it out, bastard." Ariel tore off her mask and glared at Mike, hating that smug look on his arrogant face. From the sight, she knew they were on the rooftop of the school, the area most desolated and high up in the air by four stories.

Mike didn't answer, but only signaled his followers to take action. Like a group of wolves, the pack approached the three girls with sinister grins and itchy hands.

"Don't you dare do anything funny!" Rainie screamed, grabbing onto Ariel.

"Oh, look, she's screaming!" One of the men laughed.

"She's pretty cute. Let me tell you, I want that one."

"Wei! There's only three of them and so many of us. You gotta share!"

"Bull shit! None of you will touch me!" Rainie cried. "I'll fucking bite your hands off."

"Ooo, she's feisty."

"Stay away from her!" Ariel hissed. "Touch her and I’ll kill you!"

By now, all of the men's attentions were paid to the barking Ariel and Rainie, who were all stubborn to submit defeat. Gillian found this as an opportunity to run and look for help. Surely, they would not be able to fight this on their own.

The guys grabbed onto Ariel and Rainie. The sudden touches triggered strong reactions from the girls as fright hammered them to desperation. But the guys were enjoying and laughing at their unwilling actions which were making the fun escalating even more. From aside, Mike watched with merciless amusement, loving the entertainment he was seeing.

Ariel caught his shameless amusement and repulsion thundered down her nerves. She couldn't believe what an animal Mike was. If these wolves were monsters, Mike was definitely something beyond that. Ariel could no longer bear the harassment as she harshly bit into someone's hands.

"AH!" He cried, slapping Ariel tight across the face. "How dare you!"

Ariel's eyes gleamed with infuriation and mulishness. "Biting you is even more disgusting that your nasty face," she spat.

"Why you--!" The man pulled her up by the collar of her shirt, bringing her face near to his. The exasperation suddenly ceased when he realized how flawless and smooth her skin seemed. Like an instant morph, his expression changed into that of a lascivious beast. "Ah, look at you, so pretty." With a smirk, he threw her against the rail, making her release a painful yelp.

Mike instantly became alert.

The man rubbed his hands in a perverted manner, licking his lips. He approached closer to Ariel, and she rammed against doom as fear finally found its demonstration in her face. "Don't blame me. You made me," he said, grabbing her clothes. She let out a cry, struggling, but it wasn't long before he was jerked back and got punched across the face.

"Fucking bastard," Mike spat, throwing the man to the ground. "I didn't say you can have her!"

The man wiped the blood of his face. "What are you talking about Mike? You said we can have fun--"

"Fucking shut up!" He blasted the man in the abdomen, sending him flying against the rail.

Ariel shrieked at the blood flowing out of his lips and cuts. The man glared at Mike with hatred before turning around to Ariel and pulled her up, groping her shoulders. "I'm having fun alright. What are you going to do about it?"

"You son of a bitch," Mike cursed, trouncing on the man. He gave a blast to the man's face.

Ariel watched with utter bafflement. She didn't know why Mike had lost it all of the sudden but she was somewhat grateful for his help.

"You'd better leave now before I lose my fucking patience," Mike growled, glaring penetratingly at the bruised person in his grip. Immediately, the man waved his hands and ushered his followers to leave.

Ariel glared at Mike, whose expression she could not decipher.

"Just leave."

"Don't think I'm going to be fooled by that! Are you putting up a show for me?" Ariel could not help but doubt his help.

Ariel slapped him. "I can't believe you'd stoop this low! You jerk!" She spat. "I don't want to see you ever again!" With a face red of resentment, Ariel was about to stomp off when Mike grabbed her wrists.

"Ariel--"

"Let me go!"

Suddenly, Mike was pulled backwards by a force. Ariel let out a scream when she witnessed someone blasting him across the face. That person was shockingly Joe.

"You bastard," Joe cursed, giving him another punch.

Mike slammed against the steel walls, blood dripping out of his mouth. Ariel's hands made their way towards her mouth as she watched with terror and uncertainty.

"If it isn't Joe Cheng, the cold prince," Mike said with a smirk. "You're a bit late. It was a pack of us, and we had a lot of fun--"

Joe grabbed Mike by the collar and delivered another fist to his face. Mike didn't stand to be a punching bag so he struck back, snapping Joe's head to the side. Ariel let out a horrified scream.

"Stop!" Ariel cried, running up towards them.

"Get away, Ariel!" Joe said, pushing her to the side. It was rare that he said her name but Ariel was too terrified to heed to his command. The two were still punching the daylights out of each other when Ariel decided to interfere, stopping the blow that Mike was about to send towards Joe. However, his force was far too strong for Ariel to stop it. Instead, it sent her backwards, and she was tumbling over the rail with a scream.

"Ariel!" Rainie cried, petrified.

Joe acted on instincts and immediately flew over the rail and grabbed on the edges of Ariel’s rubber gloves right in the nick of time. "Hurry! Give me your other hand!" He hissed, seeing the rubber was about to rip apart.

Ariel was too shocked at first, but she instantly recovered. She reached out her other hand and he instantly grabbed it with force. But from his expression, she knew he was having a hard time keeping her up. He was already halfway bending over. If this was to continue, she was afraid that she might pull him down with her. It wasn’t high enough to kill, but she could break several bones.

"Joe, let go. You're going to fall," Ariel cried.

"Idiot! Just hang in there," he rebuked, and then turning to the people standing behind him. "Wei! Give me a hand!"

The shocked Rainie and Mike instantly flew to his aid. Selina, Gillian, and even Jolin who had followed them here could only watch from the side, stoned and hoping that Ariel would be safe.

Luckily, the three managed to pull Ariel back up. Gillian immediately fled to Ariel, making sure she was okay.

"You stupid bastard!" Rainie hissed at Mike, delivering him a tight slap across his already bruised and wounded face. "Look at what you did!"

Mike rubbed his sore cheek, avoiding Rainie's hot glare. "I can't believe you're so low! You almost killed her, fucking jerk!"

Ariel slowed down her breathing and waved at Rainie. "Rainie just let it go--"

"No! I gotta teach this bastard! He needs to learn how to respect us women! I can't believe you'd hire a bunch of thugs to rape us! You low life dog! I don’t know what Hebe sees in you! She must be blind like Ariel has once been!"

Joe's resentful gaze lingered on Mike. Rainie's words made his inside snap as he grabbed Mike by the collar and glared into his eyes, resembling a raging demon. "You're lucky Ariel's fine. If anything had happened to her, I'd make sure you won't ever see the daylight again. Now leave!"

Ariel met Mike's gaze and she immediately turned her head away. Although he had tried to save her, she could not forgive him for what he had done, even though he hadn't meant for things to turn out this way. Mike left the scene seeing that he was not welcomed.

Selina rushed to Joe. "Are you alright?" She asked worriedly.

Joe nodded, but he could only focus on Ariel. "Are you okay?"

She absentmindedly nodded.

"Come on, I'll bring you home," Joe said supporting her. With that, he led her towards the exit, leaving Rainie, Gillian, Selina, and Jolin to look after them.

Selina could not understand what their relationship was; her gaze was a burning curiosity to know. Jolin could only watch with jealousy and rage, while both Rainie and Gillian were exchanging smiles and knowing glances.

"Damn, that girl is lucky," Rainie said.

Ariel looked up at Joe as he walked downstairs. He had a firm grip on her arm that brought her a secure feeling to her heart. They were so close she felt his genuine protection. Unknowingly, a smile was shining bright on her face.

"Thanks..." Ariel murmured.

He glanced at her, his bored expression crawling back on his face again. "What? If it was somebody else, I would have done the same thing. It's a human life, after all."

"I'm talking about right now," Ariel pointed out. "Thanks for accompanying me home."

"Stupid. We're neighbors. It makes perfect sense. I just don't want your mother and grandfather to blame me if something happens to you," Joe quickly explained, uptight.

Ariel frowned, disappointment sinking in. She was hoping that his dislike for her had somewhat defused, but it seemed that his feelings were still unchanged. He was still that aloof person who gave her the cold shoulders.

"Ow..." Ariel mumbled, finally realizing the pain in her wrist.

Joe turned to look at her, noticing the bruise on her wrist. The gloves by now were already torn, reminding him of how Ariel was on the borderline of death. All of the sudden he felt a tinge of relief and gladness bursting in his chest seeing Ariel by his side. "You're such a baby," Joe said.

Ariel bit her lips. "You grabbed me so hard..."

"Does it really hurt?" Joe asked uncertainly, feeling slightly guilty. When Ariel didn't respond, he stopped her and pulled her down to sit on the steps. "Let me see."

He studied her wrist. He felt blameworthy upon seeing the imprint of his fingers. “Why are you so heavy? If you were lighter none of it would have happened.”

She frowned at his remark. “What… I don’t weigh that much…”

Joe smirked. “Try telling that to the judge.”

“Look, you have some bruises on your face too…” Ariel took notice, hands instinctively flew to stroke them. “Aiyo, do they hurt?”

He was taken aback by her sudden touch. “These are peanuts to me,” he murmured.

“Ah!” Ariel snapped her fingers, remembering. "I think I spotted an ointment in the pockets of the apron earlier," she said, digging through it. She found it and gleamed in happiness. "I knew I could count on those old ladies to carry these things around!"

Joe rolled his eyes. "Why are you dressed this way, anyway?" He asked, snatching the ointment from her hand. Shock immediately shot through her as she realized he was going to apply the ointment on her.

"I-I..." Her mind was nowhere near normality when he gently massaged her wrist.

"Don't you girls have anything better to do? Always coming up with these stupid gimmicks. You were trying to sneak out of detention weren't you?" Joe asked.

Ariel was paying no attention to him as she watched him massage her. The softness in his eyes and tender movements threatened to make her heart soften its walls. She had never seen such a compassionate side of him.

"This going to hurt a bit..." he said, as he pressed on her bruise, making Ariel jump in pain, “just hang on..."

The pain didn't matter at all, really. As long as she could watch his gentle face she wouldn't mind suffering any sort of affliction. As he continued his delicate massage, Ariel caught herself admiring his long tall nose, his eyes, his tender lips, and prominent chin. My god, how could someone look so handsome? He was not a least bit aware of how deadly attractive he now seemed to her.

'Joe... Although he is only a fake boyfriend, I just feel that our closeness right now isn't that far off from a normal couple. You treating me with such care and consideration... how can I stop myself from falling in love with you?'
