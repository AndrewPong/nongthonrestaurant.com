About Binh Minh Quan

Established in 1998, Binh Minh Quan started off as a family restaurant serving authentic Vietnamese dishes at affordable prices. Now more than a decade later, our goal is still to serve the most authentic Vietnamese food possible and provide an excellent dining experience for the people of Oakland.

Thanks to your support, Binh Minh Quan has remodeled its home and expanded its authentic dishes. It was nominated for Best Restaurant in the San Francisco Magazine!

We also recently opened a sister restaurant in El Cerrito by the name of Nong Thon which you can promptly view by visiting the website NongThonRestaurant.Com.
